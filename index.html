Unique Selling Proposition

Coiname's unique selling proposition is centered around eliminating common issues associated with trading on the blockchain, such as gas fees, taxes, and price impact.

Bot Capabilities

The bot's ability to represent every coin on the network is intriguing. However, it's important to clarify the limitations imposed and why they are temporary. Users might want to understand the criteria for inclusion or exclusion of specific coins.

Instant Trades

The promise of almost instant buys (<0.5s) addresses the issue of waiting for long block confirmations, providing users with a faster trading experience.

Leverage Trading

The plan to introduce leverage trading without the need for collateral is a bold move. Users would likely be interested in the mechanics of this feature and the associated risks.

Revenue Model

The B&B (buys-n-burns) system for spreading revenue is explained, but you mention a possible shift to a Revenue Share system in the future. Providing more details on how these systems work and their implications for users would be beneficial.

Expansion to Multichain

The vision of expanding to non-EVM chains like SOLANA is forward-thinking. Emphasizing the benefits and challenges of extending services to different blockchains will be crucial in gaining community trust.
